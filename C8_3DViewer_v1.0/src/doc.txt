3DViewer manual

Use buttons "..." and "Open" to open .obj files.
Use sliders and spinboxes to translate and rotate models.
Use slider to scale models.
You can choose type of projection (parallel or central), type of line (solid or stipple),
type of point (none, round or square)
Also you can choose size of line and point, their colors and color of background.
Button "Save image as..." save image as .bmp or .jpeg format.
With a round slider you can change mouse sensitivity.
You can record screencasts.
At the bottom amount of vertexes and edges shown.
